Welcome to What in the Word?

The aim of the game is to guess the randomly generated word within 5 attempts.

You have the choice whether to play a 4-letter, 5-letter or 6-letter game type.

Letters that are not used in the word are coloured red.
Letters that are used in the word, but in the wrong place are coloured yellow.
Letters that are used in the word and are in the correct place are coloured green.

You can only use words that actually exist in the English Dictionary, and are the same length as the entered game mode.
